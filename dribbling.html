Dribbling Drills and Workouts

Here are some effective dribbling drills and workouts to improve your ball-handling skills:

Make sure to incoporate them into your dribbling workouts to see maximum improvement

Stationary Dribbling

Practice dribbling in place with both hands. Focus on control and maintaining a low dribble.

Duration: 1 - 2 minute per hand / workout

Figure 8 Dribbling

Set up cones in a figure 8 pattern. Dribble the ball around the cones using both hands without touching them.

Repetitions: 5 - 10 rounds / workout

Two-Ball Dribbling

Simultaneously dribble two basketballs using both hands. This drill improves coordination and ambidexterity.

Duration: 1 - 2 minutes /workout

Full-Court Dribbling

Dribble the ball from one end of the court to the other, focusing on speed and control.

Repetitions: 5 - 10 times / workout
