Shooting Drills and Workouts

Here are some effective shooting drills and workouts to improve your shooting skills:

Make sure to incoporate them into your shooting workouts to see maximum improvement

Form Shooting Drill

Focus on your shooting form with this drill. Stand close to the hoop and shoot using proper shooting mechanics - balance, hand position, and follow-through.

Repetitions: 10 - 20 shots / workout

Spot Shooting Drill

Set up shooting spots around the perimeter. Start from each spot and shoot. Move to the next spot after making a shot. This workout improves shooting from different areas.

Repetitions: 5 shots per spot, repeat at at least 5 spots / workout

Free Throw Practice

Work on your free throw shooting, an essential skill for any basketball player. Focus on consistency and proper shooting mechanics.

Repetitions: 10 - 20 free throws / workout

Off the Dribble Shooting

Practice shooting off the dribble. Dribble towards the hoop and take a jump shot or a pull-up jumper. Work on creating separation from defenders.

Repetitions: 5 shots from each side

Key things to take note of when doing these drills

  • Pay attention to your balance and stance
  • Keep your eyes on the target
  • Analyse your grip and make sure it is correct
  • Prioritise consistency in your form rather than accuracy
  • Make sure to correct your form
